Tempo keychain precompile bug

MPP services fail for passkey wallets
ERROR
mppx: internal verification error
Details: keychain precompile error:
  Account keychain error: KeyAlreadyExists
WHY
  • passkey wallets include keyAuthorization in every tx
  • Tempo precompile rejects re-submission as KeyAlreadyExists
  • field is in signed payload → cannot strip server-side
  • payment fails before it reaches the chain
FIX
  • make precompile idempotent (no-op on existing key)
  • OR wallet SDK skips keyAuthorization once key is registered
issue: github.com/tempoxyz/mpp/issues/657
